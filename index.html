About Me

Business-minded analyst with a strong data foundation.

I've always been interested in understanding how businesses grow, make decisions, and solve problems. During my MBA in Business Economics, I was introduced to the world of data and realized that behind every successful business decision is a story hidden in numbers. That curiosity led me to explore data analytics and learn tools such as SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Python.

As I worked on projects and internships, I found that I genuinely enjoy working with data. Today, I am focused on building a career in Data Analytics and Business Intelligence. I enjoy learning new technologies, solving business problems, and continuously improving my analytical skills. My goal is to use data to help organizations make smarter decisions and create a positive impact through data-driven insights.
